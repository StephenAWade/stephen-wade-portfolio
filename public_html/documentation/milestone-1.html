Purpose, Audience, and Goal of my PWP

Purpose

The main purpose of my Personal website is to provide a platform on which I can showcase my work as a creative professional in order to bolster online interaction with my name and work, attract potential patrons and admirers who are as of yet unfamiliar with my work, and to connect with interested parties who wish to contact me.

Audience

My site will be constructed with the intent:

  1. Prospective Patrons: Those who might wish to commission a piece, showcase my work in publications or concerts, or schedule a performance.
  2. Interested Fine Arts Professionals: Other members of the fine arts community who may wish to apprehend what others are doing in their field.
  3. The Casual Interested Observer: Those who wish to see/hear/read my work without expert knowledge or professional interest for the purpose of enjoyment.

Goal

It is my intention to fulfill the following goals as the result of my PWP.

  • To attract freelance clients.
  • To serve as a place to showcase my work as a writer and musician.
  • To bolster name recognition through publication.
  • To Serve as a place to develop and showcase web-development skills for potential future employers.

Persona

Name

Samantha Klein (50 years old)

Profession

Samantha is an editor for a regional fine arts magazine based in Santa Fe, New Mexico.

Technologies, Behaviors/Attitudes, and Goals

Technologies

Samantha uses a 13-inch, 1.6 GHz Dual-Core Intel Core i5 Macbook Pro with the macOSS Monterey Version 12.2.1. She uses Google Chrome. She is a power user who works in a fast-paced literary environment in which she is required to read pieces from her devices, provide edits, and submit them back to their authors for changes. She also uses her devices to interact with agents and to locate and interact with unrepresented talent. She has an iPhone 13 on which she sends SMS texts and emails (also using it for a hot spot in the even that she does not have access to wireless internet), and an iPad mini on which she reads pieces during travel (or as a break from her computer).
