About Me

Raised in the mountains of Northern New Mexico, Stephen at an early age displayed an unusual aptitude for classical music, a pursuit which has lead him to study and perform with a number of distinguished and acclaimed musicians from all over the globe. In addition to music, Stephen has developed a deep passion for literature and philosophy, studying with some of the most well respected thinkers and authors of his day. Stephen now seeks to utilize the tools and talents he has developed to encourage others to think critically about the world around them and to flourish as they discover and apply their own creative and academic gifts.

Creative Writing

Stephen has long harbored a passion for communication and literature. Stephen currently works as a Freelance writer creating content for online publication and is the author of the blog “Let’s Think…” where he explores issues in politics, religion, music, and literature, in an attempt to foster thought-provoking dialogue, encourage a critical approach to complex issues, as well as to meaningfully interact with the art community as a whole. Stephen also writes literary fiction and is preparing a number of manuscripts for circulation in 2022.
